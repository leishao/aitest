YouTube Podcast to Article

Generate articles from podcast transcripts

Paste a YouTube URL, pick a style, and get a structured article based on the transcript.

How it works

  1. Paste the YouTube podcast link.
  2. Choose a writing style and length.
  3. Generate the transcript and article.

Tip: Provide a custom style note for tone, audience, or format.

Captions must be available for the video.
